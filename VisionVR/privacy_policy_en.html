EyeState Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 24, 2026

We value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how EyeState (“the App”) handles data when you use the app.

1. Do We Collect Your Data

The App does not upload or collect any personal information or test results to the developer or any server. All tests and results are processed and stored only on your device. We do not have access to any of your data.

2. How Data Is Used Within the App

Test results and similar data are stored only on your device and used to display your results and history within the app. We do not use, store, or access this data, and we do not share it with third parties or use it for advertising.

3. Data Security

All data remains only on your device and is not transmitted over the internet. Please keep your device and account secure to protect your local data.

4. Your Rights

Test results are stored only on your device. You may delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app’s data.
