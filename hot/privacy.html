Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

StreakMate helps users find and connect with matching partners for third-party social platforms. We value your privacy and aim to collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide matching, safety, subscription, and notification features.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Depending on how you use StreakMate, you may provide:

  • Third-party social platform usernames or identifiers that you choose to add.
  • Your gender and matching preferences.
  • Reports, block actions, feedback, and other information that you voluntarily submit.

Matching and App Activity

We may process information related to your use of StreakMate, including:

  • Matching requests and whether a request was sent or received.
  • Match status, including pending, accepted, declined, or expired requests.
  • Selected social platform and matching preferences.
  • Match history and request timestamps.
  • Reports, blocks, and other safety-related actions.
  • App activity and diagnostic information used to operate and improve the service.

Device and Technical Information

We may collect or process limited technical information, including:

  • App or device identifiers used to distinguish an app installation.
  • Device language and time zone.
  • App version and operating system information.
  • IP address, request timestamps, and server logs.
  • Diagnostic and error information.
  • Push notification registration tokens if notifications are enabled.

Subscription Information

If you subscribe to StreakMate Pro, purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store.

We may receive subscription status and entitlement information required to unlock premium matching features.

We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain StreakMate.
  • Find potential matching partners.
  • Apply available matching preferences.
  • Manage sent and received match requests.
  • Display platform usernames after the applicable matching process has been completed.
  • Maintain match history.
  • Provide report and block functionality.
  • Verify StreakMate Pro subscription entitlements.
  • Send service-related push notifications when enabled.
  • Detect abuse, fraud, or misuse of the service.
  • Investigate reports and safety issues.
  • Diagnose technical problems and improve the app.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How Matching Information Is Shared

StreakMate is designed to help users connect through third-party social platforms.

Information you provide for matching may be displayed to another StreakMate user as part of the matching process.

Before a match is accepted, a social platform username may be partially hidden.

The complete platform username may be displayed after the applicable matching or acceptance process has been completed.

StreakMate does not provide in-app private messaging. Conversations that take place on Snapchat, Instagram, Telegram, Discord, TikTok, or other third-party platforms are governed by the terms and privacy practices of those platforms.

4. Push Notifications

StreakMate may use Firebase Cloud Messaging to deliver service-related push notifications.

If notifications are enabled, a push notification registration token may be processed to deliver notifications to your device.

Notifications may include information about matching requests or changes to match status.

You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We may use third-party service providers to operate StreakMate. These providers may process information on our behalf only as necessary to provide their services.

Service providers may include:

  • Cloudflare for infrastructure, API hosting, databases, networking, and security.
  • Google Firebase for push notification delivery.
  • Apple for App Store subscription and purchase processing.

These providers may process information according to their own applicable privacy policies and legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide StreakMate, maintain matching records, protect users, prevent abuse, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of information.

Safety information related to reports or blocked users may be retained for a longer period when reasonably necessary to prevent repeated abuse or protect the service.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures designed to protect information against unauthorized access, misuse, loss, or alteration.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Privacy Choices and Rights

Depending on your country or region, you may have rights regarding your personal information.

These rights may include the right to:

  • Request access to information associated with your use of StreakMate.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of information.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing where applicable.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

StreakMate does not require a traditional user account. Therefore, we may request limited technical information, such as an app or device identifier, to locate data associated with your installation and verify a privacy request.

9. Children

StreakMate is not intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Where local law requires a higher minimum age or additional parental consent, applicable local legal requirements may apply.

If you believe a child has provided information to StreakMate in violation of this Privacy Policy, please contact us so that we can review the matter and take appropriate action.

10. Third-Party Platforms

StreakMate may help you find users for or open third-party social platforms.

We do not own, operate, or control Snapchat, Instagram, Telegram, Discord, TikTok, or other third-party services.

We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, communications, or activities that occur on third-party platforms.

You should review the terms and privacy policies of each third-party platform before using that service or sharing personal information.

11. International Data Processing

StreakMate may use service providers that process information in countries other than the country where you live.

Where required by applicable law, we take reasonable steps designed to process information in accordance with applicable data protection requirements.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

When changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date shown at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
