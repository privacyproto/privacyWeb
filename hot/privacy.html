Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to StreakMate. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

This Privacy Policy explains how StreakMate handles information when you use the app, its Home Screen widgets, Lock Screen widgets, notifications, and related features.

StreakMate is designed as a local utility. It does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use its core features.

2. Information We Collect

StreakMate does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, messages, or social media credentials.

You do not need to sign in to Snapchat, TikTok, QQ, or any other third-party platform through StreakMate.

3. Information Stored on Your Device

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Platforms you choose to enable;
  • Notification times and notification text;
  • Daily completion status;
  • App preferences and interface settings;
  • Information needed to display Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets.

This information is used only to provide the app’s functionality. It is not uploaded to our servers.

4. Notifications

With your permission, StreakMate may send local notifications at the times you select.

Notification scheduling is handled by the operating system on your device. You can disable or change notification permission at any time in your device settings.

5. Widgets

StreakMate provides Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets that display selected platform names and daily completion status.

Widget information is shared locally between the main app and its widget extension through Apple-provided system storage. This information is not transmitted to us.

6. Third-Party Platforms

StreakMate may display the names or icons of supported third-party platforms solely to help you organize your daily interactions.

StreakMate is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or officially associated with Snapchat, TikTok, QQ, Kuaishou, or any other third-party platform.

We do not access your accounts, messages, friend lists, activity history, passwords, or content from those platforms.

7. Data Sharing and Sale

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with advertisers, data brokers, or other third parties.

Because the app’s core information remains on your device, we do not have access to your daily completion records or platform selections.

8. Tracking and Advertising

StreakMate does not use your information to track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

The app does not use your platform selections or interaction status for targeted advertising.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Information created in StreakMate remains on your device until you change it, reset the app’s data, or delete the app.

You may delete locally stored information by removing the relevant settings within the app or by uninstalling StreakMate.

Since StreakMate does not require an account and does not upload this information to our servers, there is no separate online account or server-side profile to delete.

10. Children’s Privacy

StreakMate does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not provide public profiles, messaging, random matching, or other features that allow users to communicate with strangers.

Parents or guardians who believe that a child has provided personal information to us may contact us using the email address below.

11. Security

We use the security features provided by iOS to help protect information stored by the app.

However, no device, software, or storage method can be guaranteed to be completely secure. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and system account.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app’s features or legal requirements change.

Any updated version will be published on this page with a revised “Last updated” date. Continued use of the app after an update means that you acknowledge the revised policy.

13. Contact Us
