Privacy Policy

Welcome to “IsleSpace” (the “App”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not actively collect, upload, or analyze any personally identifiable information.

All content you create within the App (such as text, images, and records) is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account for synchronization and backup purposes.

2. iCloud Synchronization

The App uses Apple’s iCloud / CloudKit services to synchronize and share data.

All data storage, transmission, and access are handled by Apple’s iCloud services. The App does not have access to your Apple ID information.

3. Data Sharing

Your data will not be shared with any third party unless you explicitly invite others through the App’s sharing features.

The App does not sell, rent, or trade your data to any third parties.

4. Data Security

We rely on Apple iCloud’s security mechanisms, including encrypted transmission and storage, to protect your data.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through the App or update notes.
