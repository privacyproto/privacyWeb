Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

KidGate ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information of our users. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected and how it is used when you use the KidGate application.

Information We Collect

KidGate does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Home Address
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Messages

Local Data Storage

To provide parental control features, KidGate may store certain information locally on your device, including:

  • Website Allow Lists
  • Website Block Lists
  • Screen Time Settings
  • Browsing Activity Records
  • Parent Control Preferences

This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics

KidGate collects limited anonymous usage information to help improve app performance and understand overall product usage.

This information may include:

  • App Launch Events
  • First Install Events
  • App Version
  • Operating System Version
  • Device Model
  • General Usage Statistics

This information is used solely for analytics, performance monitoring, and product improvement. It is not used to identify individual users and is not used for advertising purposes.

Children's Privacy

KidGate is designed to help families create a safer browsing experience for children.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Browsing history and parental control settings are stored locally on the device unless otherwise stated.

Third-Party Services

KidGate may use Apple services necessary for application functionality, including:

  • App Store Services
  • Screen Time APIs
  • Family Controls Framework

These services are governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information handled by the application. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may remove locally stored data at any time by deleting app data or uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and reflected by the updated date above.
