Space Sharing Tutorial

Share spaces via iCloud and view moments together with family or friends.

Before You Start

  • Your device must be signed in to iCloud (Apple ID); sharing requires iCloud.

Start Sharing (Space Owner)

1️⃣ Open the timeline of a space you created from the home screen.
2️⃣ Tap the “More” menu (⋯) in the top-right, then choose “Shared”.
3️⃣ On the sharing screen, tap “Generate Invitation Link” or “Get Link”.

You can do either of the following:

Option A: Anyone with the link can join
Select “Share Options”
Enable “Allow anyone with the link to add people”
Go back and tap “Copy Link”, then send the link to the person you’re inviting
Option B: Share with specific people
Select “Share with more people”
Choose how to send the invite (Messages, Mail, etc.)
Enter their Apple ID, tap “Done” on the keyboard, then tap “Share Link”
📌 Note:
• Only spaces you created show the “Shared” option; spaces shared with you do not.
• After the link is created, anyone who opens it can accept the invite. Once accepted, the space appears on their home screen.

Accept an Invite (Invitee)

1️⃣ After receiving the share link, copy it and open the app.
2️⃣ On the home screen, tap the “+” in the top-right, choose “Join Space”, paste the link into the field, and tap “Join”.
3️⃣ If the space doesn’t appear right away, send the app to the background and reopen it. If it still doesn’t show, try the join steps again.

FAQ

❓ Opening the link says I don’t have permission?
👉 Make sure you’re signed in to this app and iCloud with the Apple ID that was invited.
❓ The link only opens a webpage in Safari?
👉 Tap “Open in App” on the page or in the browser to accept the invite in this app.
❓ How do I leave a shared space?
👉 In System Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Account → Sharing you can stop participating, or ask the space owner to remove your access.
